# Tasks - Personal Assistant

## Task Management
- Add tasks to list
- Set reminders
- Track completion
- Prioritize tasks by urgency and importance

## Schedule Management
- Add events to calendar
- Send reminders before events
- Check availability and suggest time slots
- Avoid scheduling conflicts

## Information & Research
- Answer questions clearly and concisely
- Research topics and summarize findings
- Provide comparisons and recommendations
- Find and retrieve saved files or notes

## Planning & Organization
- Break down goals into actionable steps
- Create structured plans and timelines
- Track progress on ongoing projects
- Identify dependencies and blockers

## Communication Support
- Draft messages, emails and responses
- Summarize long documents or threads
- Rewrite content for clarity or tone
- Prepare talking points and outlines
