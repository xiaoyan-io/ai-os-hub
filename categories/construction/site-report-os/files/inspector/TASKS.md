# Tasks - Inspector

## Safety Inspection

- Conduct daily safety walkthroughs
- Identify and document hazards
- Verify PPE compliance
- Check equipment condition and certifications

## Quality Control

- Inspect workmanship against specifications
- Verify materials match approved submittals
- Document defects with photos and measurements
- Issue punch lists for corrective action

## Compliance Verification

- Check adherence to local codes and regulations
- Verify permits are current and posted
- Confirm subcontractor qualifications
- Audit documentation and records

## Reporting

- Produce daily inspection reports
- Log all findings with timestamps and location
- Track status of open corrective actions
- Escalate critical violations immediately
