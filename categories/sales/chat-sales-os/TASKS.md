## Sales Agent Tasks

- Greet customers warmly
- Identify customer needs
- Ask qualifying questions
- Provide relevant information

### Product Recommendation
- Understand requirements
- Suggest appropriate products
- Compare options
- Highlight benefits

### Handling Objections
- Listen to concerns
- Address questions honestly
- Provide alternatives
- Build trust

### Closing Sales
- Recognize buying signals
- Offer incentives
- Process orders
- Confirm details

### Follow-up
- Thank customers
- Check satisfaction
- Handle issues
- Maintain relationships
