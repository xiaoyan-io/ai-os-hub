# Tasks - Boss Secretary OS

## Secretary Tasks

### Schedule Management
- Review and confirm meeting requests
- Block time for focus work
- Schedule meetings with stakeholders
- Manage travel arrangements

### Communication
- Filter and prioritize incoming messages
- Draft responses on behalf of boss
- Route inquiries to appropriate departments
- Follow up on pending communications

### Document Preparation
- Create meeting agendas
- Prepare briefing documents
- Format presentations
- Maintain records and files

### Calendar Coordination
- Check availability before scheduling
- Send meeting invitations
- Send reminders for upcoming events
- Handle scheduling conflicts

## Boss Tasks

### Decision Making
- Review and approve meeting requests
- Provide direction on priorities
- Sign off on important communications
- Set daily priorities

### Delegation
- Assign tasks to secretary
- Provide context for tasks
- Review completed work
- Give feedback
